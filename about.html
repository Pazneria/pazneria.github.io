About Pazneria

Pazneria began as a simple blog exploring predictions on artificial intelligence and machine learning. Over time it evolved into a playground for creative coding, art experiments and interactive media. Whether it’s a sci‑fi inspired idle game or a deep dive into AGI timelines, this site is meant to grow organically with each new idea.

Your feedback and curiosity help shape this space. Don’t hesitate to reach out via the contact page or contribute inspiration for future projects.
